GymLock

Privacy Policy
Effective: May 2026

GymLock ("the app") is an Android application that helps you stay productive by locking selected apps until you complete physical exercise (push-ups or steps). This policy explains what data the app accesses and how it is handled.

1. Data We Collect

We do not collect, transmit, or share any personal data. All information used by the app is stored locally on your device and never leaves it.

2. Permissions Used

  • Accessibility Service — Used solely to detect which app is in the foreground so the app-lock feature can work. The accessibility service does not read screen content, passwords, or any text. It only observes app-switch events.
  • Camera — Used only during push-up sessions to run on-device pose detection (ML Kit). Camera frames are processed in real time on your device and are never stored or transmitted.
  • Activity Recognition — Used to count steps via the device accelerometer during walking sessions. Step data is only stored locally as a time-bank balance.
  • Foreground Service / Wake Lock — Used to keep step counting active while the screen is locked, so your progress is not lost when you pocket your phone.
  • Post Notifications — Used to show an ongoing notification during active step-counting sessions (required by Android for foreground services).

3. Data Storage

The app stores the following data locally on your device using Android SharedPreferences:

  • List of apps you have chosen to lock
  • Your current time-bank balance (in seconds)
  • The date of the last daily bonus grant

This data never leaves your device. There are no servers, no accounts, and no analytics.

4. Third-Party Services

The app uses Google ML Kit Pose Detection (bundled, runs fully offline) for push-up counting. No data is sent to Google during pose detection. See ML Kit Terms of Service for more information.

5. Children's Privacy

GymLock is not directed at children under 13. We do not knowingly collect any data from children.

6. Changes to This Policy

If this policy changes, the updated version will be posted at this URL with a new effective date.
